# The Floor is Lava Design Document

A Halo Infinite Slayer game mode where not staying airborne will damage you.

## Game Mode

- Slayer:Arena
  - Normal Slayer scoring and time rules
  - Not strictly for slayer, could be adapted to other modes
- Forge Mode; scripted logic
- 4–24 Players
- Single-round, but code support for multi-round

## Maps

- Any map intended for core multiplayer PvP
- Preferrably maps with high verticality

## Audience

- Casual players
- Minigame enjoyers

## Gameplay loop

- Players spawn in and are launched in the air to give an indication of wanting to stay airborne and off the ground
- If players land on the ground (are not airborne), they will rapidly start taking damage
- Players attempt to constantly stay airborne while fighting enemies
